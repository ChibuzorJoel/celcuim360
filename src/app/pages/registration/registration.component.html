Terms & Conditions

Please read the following terms carefully before registering for the Celcium360 Solutions Work Readiness Program.

1. Enrollment & Eligibility

Registration is open to final year students, recent graduates, NYSC corps members, graduates awaiting NYSC posting, and early-career professionals. All submitted information must be accurate and truthful. Falsified documents or information will result in immediate disqualification without a refund.

2. Payment

Payment must be made via bank transfer only to the account details provided. The applicable fee is determined by your selected category:

  • NYSC / Awaiting Posting: ₦50,000 (requires verified documents)
  • Graduate / Professional: ₦200,000

Payment is non-refundable once enrollment is confirmed. If your registration is rejected due to invalid documents, you will be contacted for re-submission within the same cohort at no additional cost.

3. Document Verification

All submitted documents — including your Statement of Result, Degree Certificate, NYSC Call-Up Letter, and payment receipt — will be verified. Enrollment is provisional until verification is complete (within 24 hours on business days).

4. Live Photo (Headshot)

A live headshot captured during registration is required for identity verification. This image will be stored securely and linked to your profile. It will not be shared with third parties.

5. Program Participation

Enrolled participants are expected to attend scheduled live virtual classes (Saturdays 2:00 PM WAT and Sundays 4:00 PM WAT). Attendance is tracked. Persistent absence without notice may result in suspension from the cohort.

6. Intellectual Property

All program materials, curriculum content, recordings, and resources provided are the intellectual property of Celcium360 Solutions. Participants may not reproduce, distribute, or resell any content without written permission.

7. Data Privacy

Your personal information and uploaded documents are collected solely for enrollment verification and program delivery. We do not sell or share your data with third parties. Data is stored securely in accordance with applicable privacy regulations.

8. Code of Conduct

Participants must maintain professional conduct during all sessions. Harassment, disruptive behaviour, or plagiarism will result in removal from the program without a refund.

9. Contact

For questions regarding these terms, contact us at contact@celcium360solutions.com
